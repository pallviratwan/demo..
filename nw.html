Healthy Fruit Salad Recipe

Try this refreshing fruit salad recipe:
  1. chop your Favorite fruits,including apple,bananas and strawberries.
  2. mix them in a bowl and add a squeeze of fresh orange juice.
  3. Top with a handful of grapes and enjoy!
